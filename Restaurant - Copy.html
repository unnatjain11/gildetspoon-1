About Us

Gilded Spoon is a vibrant culinary destination in Phagwara that celebrates the art of cooking and the joy of dining. Our restaurant is a fusion of modern elegance and traditional charm, offering a unique atmosphere for food enthusiasts. We take pride in sourcing the freshest ingredients and crafting dishes that tantalize the taste buds. Whether you're here for a casual meal or a special occasion, our dedicated team is committed to providing an unforgettable dining experience.
